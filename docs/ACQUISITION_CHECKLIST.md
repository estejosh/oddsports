# Audience Acquisition Checklist (opt-in only)

No purchased/scraped lists — CAN-SPAM/GDPR exposure, deliverability damage, and risk to Betchu's license. Everything below builds an opt-in funnel.

## Week 1–2: foundations
- [ ] Beehiiv signup page live with lead magnet ("Bankroll Management 101" PDF or public pick tracker)
- [ ] Free Telegram broadcast channel created, listed in Telegram directories
- [ ] X/Twitter account: daily free card + 1 deep-dive thread/week (threads → newsletter CTA)
- [ ] Public performance page (CLV record) — the single best trust asset

## Week 3–6: community presence
- [ ] Reddit: r/sportsbook (~2M), r/sportsbetting, r/dfsports — value-first posts (line-movement writeups, CLV recaps), newsletter in profile/signature only; read each sub's self-promo rules first
- [ ] Discord: 3–5 betting/DFS servers; offer free Starter trials to members, AMAs with mods' blessing
- [ ] Newsletter recommendation swaps with adjacent sports newsletters (Beehiiv recommendations)

## Week 6+: paid & partnerships
- [ ] Beehiiv Boosts (paid cross-newsletter acquisition) — start small, track CAC vs LTV
- [ ] X/Reddit ads test budget timed to NFL kickoff (September = biggest acquisition window)
- [ ] **Betchu existing customers** — highest-quality seed list; BLOCKED on legal confirming Betchu's privacy policy/opt-in terms permit cross-promotion emails

## Metrics to watch
- Signup conversion by channel (UTM everything)
- Free → paid rate per cohort (target 3–5% / 30d)
- Email deliverability (spam rate < 0.1%, keep Beehiiv sending reputation clean)
